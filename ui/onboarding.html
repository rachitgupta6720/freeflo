Voice, on-device

Talk to your machine. The words appear.

freeflo turns speech into text anywhere on your Mac — hold a key, speak, release. Transcription runs entirely on-device. No cloud, no API keys, nothing typed leaves this machine.

System access

Two permissions to work.

macOS gates the microphone and keyboard for your safety. Grant these once and freeflo can listen and type at your cursor.

Microphone

Capture audio while you hold the key. Recording is temporary — deleted the instant it's transcribed.

Your data, your call

Help freeflo improve — only if you want.

To keep freeflo reliable and free, we'd like two anonymous signals. You decide now, and can change it anytime in Settings.

Anonymous usage analytics

Counts and app/OS version tied to a random ID — never your transcriptions. Shows us how many people rely on freeflo.

Crash reports

When freeflo crashes, send the technical stack trace so we can fix it. No audio, no text — just what broke.

Your words never leave this Mac.

Analytics and crash reports carry metadata only. Transcribed text and audio are always excluded — a guarantee, not a setting.
